You are checking whether a candidate FOL formula is a plausible translation of
a natural language sentence.

Given:
  NL: {nl_sentence}
  FOL candidate: {candidate_fol}

Answer with a JSON object:
{
  "plausible": <true | false>,
  "reason": "<one sentence explanation>"
}

A candidate is PLAUSIBLE if:
  1. The predicates used are semantically appropriate for the sentence.
  2. The quantifier structure matches the NL quantification.
  3. No predicate is obviously hallucinated or wrong.

A candidate is NOT PLAUSIBLE if:
  1. Key entities or properties from the NL are missing.
  2. The wrong quantifier is used (e.g., universal where existential is needed).
  3. A predicate clearly contradicts the sentence meaning.

Output JSON only, no markdown.
